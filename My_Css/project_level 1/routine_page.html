My daily routine

Morning routine

  • Drink a full glass of water to rehydrate after sleep.
  • Gentle stretching, yoga, or a short workout to wake up your body.
  • Meditation, deep breathing, or expressing gratitude to center yourself.
  • Enjoy a healthy breakfast with protein, fats, and carbs to fuel your day.
  • Get natural light exposure to regulate your sleep cycle.
  • Make your bed and plan your top priorities for the day.
  • Dedicate time to self-care, reading, or something enjoyable before work

Afternoon routine

  • Enjoy a balanced meal, tidy your workspace, refill water, and take 5-10 minutes to relax or listen to music.
  • Get fresh air with a walk, stretch, or a quick workout to boost oxygen and focus.
  • Work in 25-minute sprints with 5-minute breaks for deep tasks like studying or important projects
  • Tackle less demanding activities like email, life admin, or brainstorming when energy dips
  • Prepare for the next day by planning 6 key tasks or simply reflecting and practicing gratitude

Evening routine

  • Incorporate mindful movement or yoga to release physical tensions.
  • A short walk after dinner aids digestion and clears the mind.
  • Take a warm shower or bath to relax muscles and lower your core temperature for sleep
  • Turn off electronic devices (phones, TVs) at least 60 minutes before bed to avoid sleep-disrupting blue light.
  • Use a "brain dump" to clear anxious thoughts or a gratitude journal to celebrate daily wins.
